Vinh Long

Vinh Long, where I was born and grew up, is a small town in south of Vietnam. It locates between Tien River and Hau River which reiforece alluvium for argriculture and supply water for aquiculture. There are around one and half milion people in this 1,475 square kilometer area. Vinh Long is divided in six districts, which are:

  • Binh Minh
  • Binh Tan
  • Long Ho
  • Mang Thit
  • Tam Binh
  • Tra On
  • Vung Liem

Because of surounding by rivers, boats, cannos, and ferry-boats had become the main transpot at Vinh Long. Hoverower, they are have beean replacing by road vehicle since infastruture were improve. The most famous architechture is My Thuan Bridge which is the longest and most beautiful bridge in Vinh Long. It was built by Bilfinger Berger and completed in 2000. My thuan has made traffic flow better, and it also cconnects people from both side of Tien River. Vinh Long is famous for eco-tourism zone with many kind of tropical fruits and fresh air.
